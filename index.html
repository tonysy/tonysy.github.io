Songyang Zhang

Young Scientist at Shanghai AI Laboratory, Shanghai, China.

I am a Young Scientist of Shanghai AI Laboratory, Large Model Center, collaborate with Dr. Kai Chen. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher, supervised by Prof. Dahua Lin. Currently, I lead a team working on foundation model, includes the research and open-source platform. My team to initiate the OpenCompass project, an evaluation and analysi platform for foundation model.

My team also contributes to the InternLM and InternVL, working on the research and open-source of large language model/vision-language model. We also developed OpenMMLab projects MMPreTrain.

I obtained Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Chinese Academy of Science(UCAS), in the joint program at PLUS Lab, ShanghaiTech Univeristy in 2022. I got my B.Sc. degree in 2017 from Beihang University.

Open positions include full-time researchers/engineers and interns, feel free to contact me through the email. Research directions include: Post-training and Alignment of LLMs/VLMs, Evaluation and Analysis of Foundation Model, Data-centric AI, etc.
